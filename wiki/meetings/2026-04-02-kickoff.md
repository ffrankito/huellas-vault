## Context

First meeting with the Huellas de Paz team. Two-part session: first half covered business strategy and the decision to build independently of Jaque Mate; second half went deep on the product roadmap, services, plans, and operational roles.

---

## Key decisions

### Build independent of Jaque Mate

Huellas operates on Jaque Mate (their main veterinary management system), but the owners chose to build Mascotas as a fully independent unit. Reasons:

- Jaque Mate controls the database and all processes — changes require their approval or wait cycles
- The owning family has used Jaque Mate for 15+ years and has low tolerance for disruption to it
- The goal is for Mascotas to function as a **separate cell** that demonstrates value before any future migration discussion

**Implication for the build:** CRM is standalone. Jaque Mate integration is export-only (not real-time). See [[wiki/decisions/2026-04-jaque-mate-independencia]].

### Phased delivery

The team agreed on a versioned delivery approach based on Ravenna's prior experience with NeoVet. No big-bang launch — each phase adds a layer on top of a stable foundation. The roadmap defined in this meeting maps directly to the phases in [[wiki/domain/modelo-dominio]].

### Window of opportunity: 3-month head start

Franco noted that Huellas has the **only formal crematorio license in Rosario** at this point. Competitors exist but can't legally use the word "crematorio". The team agreed to launch the landing fast (April/May) to capture leads before competitors consolidate — even before the cremation oven arrives (2–2.5 months out).

**Implication:** Fase 0 landing would show plans and a contact form but **no cotizador** (cremations not yet operational). The cotizador was built but intentionally hidden until the oven is installed.

### Plans structure defined here

Three plan tiers covering one pet, with deferred coverage:
- 0% coverage months 1–6
- 50% coverage months 7–12
- 100% from month 13

Additional pets: +50% on the base fee per extra pet.

**Note:** Specific plan names and prices were left as TBD pending final client decision. Placeholders remain in landing/cotizador.
