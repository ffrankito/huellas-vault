# Domain

Business domain knowledge — not code, not decisions. The "how the business works" layer.

## What belongs here

- Service types and their rules (cremación individual, comunitaria, entierro)
- Plan coverage logic (0% / 50% / 100% by month, mascota adicional +50%)
- Cremation workflow and operational steps
- Pricing logic and fee structures
- Roles and permissions from a business perspective (not technical)

## What does NOT belong here

- Tech decisions → `decisions/`
- Feature specs → `features/`
- People profiles → `people/`
